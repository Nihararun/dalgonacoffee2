☕ Dalgona Coffee Recipe ☕

This yummy South Korean coffee treat that's all over the internet only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard – you feel like you’ve won the jackpot, right? Dalgona coffee is a beverage made by whipping equal proportions of instant coffee powder, sugar, and hot water until it becomes creamy and then adding it to cold or hot milk.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • istant coffee
  • hot water
  • milk

Dalgona making process

step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons (tbsp) of instant coffee, 2 tbsp of sugar, and 2 tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing

step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.

step 3: Serve with Milk:
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw, and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
